My Life

My life is all about art and beauty.

Music

My music journey started with digital keyboard when I was 5. Afterwards, I learned the accordion and the violin. At college, I transitioned to the piano to keep advancing my keyboard skills and took a beginner Ruan class to get into Chinese instruments. I love classical music and film scores the most, and I hope to get into the field of music arrangement one day.

Travel

Travelling is always the most exciting thing throughout my holidays, no matter near or far. I love the nature so much and I think travelling is a good way to get into the nature - exploring the geography, the astronomy, the plants, the weather... I've travlled to most of the provinces in China but not many international trips. My next step is to travel around the US.

Dance

I was once a ballet learner throughout my childhood but have been away from it after finishing junior high school. So I'm now a huge ballet fan but not a dancer any more:( The good news is I'm easing back to it recently and hope to take some in person classes after covid. My dream is to perform some classical variations on pointe again on the stage in the future.

Plants

I enjoy growing plants because they can brighten up my home and cheer me up! At my home in China, I grew vegetables and fruits on the balcony to enjoy fresh foods. During my undergraduate, I grew different kinds of flowers in my dorm. Now in the US, however, all my attempts to grow plants from seeds failed:( So I'm now finding my ways to fresh cut flowers.

Pets

I love pets, but I haven't got any in my life so far:( For the question cats or dogs, I love them equally, but it seems I've become more of a cat person than a dog person after following lots of cat vloggers. Due to many limitations, I can only "cloud raise" a cat on the Internet. (Thanks tech!) I hope that I can adopt a cat when I settle down to work after graduation.

Cook

Everyone loves food, but not anyone can cook. I'm also a newbie in cooking, but I'm working hard to advance my cooking skills. I like Asian food the most, such as Chinese food (Cantonese food is the best!) and sushi. I also enjoy watching baking tutorials to see how the delicated pieces are made, but always failed when doing on my own:(
